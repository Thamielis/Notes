Remove-NcSystemHypervisorUplinkMapping
--------------------------------------

### Synopsis
Destroy the hypervisor uplink mapping.

---

### Description

Destroy the hypervisor uplink mapping.

---

### Examples
> Example 1

Remove-NcSystemHypervisorUplinkMapping -Node sapanj-vsim3 -Port 8888
Destroy the hypervisor uplink mapping.
