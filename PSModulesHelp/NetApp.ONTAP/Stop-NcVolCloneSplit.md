Stop-NcVolCloneSplit
--------------------

### Synopsis
Stop the process of splitting off a clone from its parent volume and snapshot.

---

### Description

Stop the process of splitting off a clone from its parent volume and snapshot.  All of the blocks that were formerly shared between the given clone and its parent volume that have already been split off will remain that way.

---

* [Get-NcVolCloneSplit](Get-NcVolCloneSplit)

### Examples
> Example 1

```PowerShell
Stop-NcVolCloneSplit vol2clone
Stop splitting volume clone 'vol2clone' from its parent volume.
```
